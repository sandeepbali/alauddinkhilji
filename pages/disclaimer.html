Legal disclaimer, editorial policy, and source methodology for this archive.

General Disclaimer

This website, alauddinkhilji.com, is an educational resource created and maintained as part of the Bharat Files Initiative. The content presented here is intended solely for educational and informational purposes. The aim is to present historically documented facts about the reign of Alauddin Khilji (1296–1316 CE) based on primary historical sources, peer-reviewed scholarship, and archaeological evidence.

Historical Accuracy

Every effort has been made to ensure the historical accuracy of the information presented on this website. All claims are sourced from:

  • Primary historical texts written by contemporary or near-contemporary chroniclers, including Ziauddin Barani (Tarikh-i-Firoz Shahi), Amir Khusrau (Khazain-ul-Futuh, Tarikh-i-Alai), and Isami (Futuh-us-Salatin).
  • Modern academic scholarship by recognized historians including K.S. Lal, R.C. Majumdar, Richard Eaton, Irfan Habib, and Abraham Eraly.
  • Archaeological evidence documented by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) and other recognized bodies.

A comprehensive list of all sources is available on our Sources & References page. Readers are encouraged to independently verify all claims using the cited sources.

Editorial Policy

This website presents historical events as documented in primary sources. Our editorial approach is guided by the following principles:

  • Source-first: Every factual claim is tied to a specific historical source, cited with footnotes linking to our references page.
  • Transparency: Where historians disagree on interpretations, we acknowledge the debate and present the primary source evidence.
  • Context: Events are presented within their historical context, not in isolation.
  • No fabrication: We do not present unsourced claims, legends, or folklore as historical fact. Where we reference cultural narratives (such as the Padmavati legend from Jayasi's poem), we clearly distinguish them from documented history.

Not Intended to Promote Hatred

This website documents historical events for educational purposes. It is not intended to promote hatred, hostility, or discrimination against any community, religion, or group. The actions of historical rulers do not reflect upon their descendants or modern followers of any faith.

The purpose of this archive is to ensure that documented historical truths are accessible to all Indians, so that the sacrifices and suffering of our ancestors are neither forgotten nor minimized. Understanding history — even its most painful chapters — is essential for building a more informed and just society.
